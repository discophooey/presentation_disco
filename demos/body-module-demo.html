The Investigation Unfolds

For months, our team pored over thousands of documents, conducting interviews with former employees and analyzing financial records that had never before been made public.

What We Found

The pattern was unmistakable. Year after year, funds designated for community programs were being redirected through a complex web of shell companies, each transaction carefully obscured behind layers of bureaucratic paperwork.

When we confronted officials with our findings, they initially denied any wrongdoing. But as the evidence mounted, their story began to change.
